Bone Health Guide

Maintaining strong bones for a healthy life

Eat calcium-rich foods and get your daily dose of Vitamin D

It's essential for maintaining strong bones. Avoid smoking and heavy drinking!

Engage in weight-bearing exercises

Regular physical activity keeps bones strong and improves muscle strength.

Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol

These habits can weaken your bones and overall health.

Get your bone mineral density tested

Regular testing helps monitor bone health and prevent fractures.

Cut back on refined sugar

Excess sugar can negatively impact your bones' health.

Making bone-healthy choices can help you keep your bones strong for life!

Exercise, healthy food, and lifestyle choices matter for strong bones.
